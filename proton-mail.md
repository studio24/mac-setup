# Proton mail setup guide

## Installing the app
- Once you have created your Proton account, you can download the Proton Mail app from within your [Proton account](https://account.proton.me/u/9/mail/get-the-apps)
- Open the downloaded file and follow the installation instructions

## Setup
1. Open the app, this can be found via Alfred by searching for `mail`
2. Login with your Proton account details

**NOTE** The below links take you to the Proton web interface to set these things up, however, they can also be set within the Mail application.

[Identity and Addresses](https://account.proton.me/u/9/mail/identity-addresses)

To set your 
- Display name and signature
- addresses and aliases

[Forward and auto reply](https://account.proton.me/u/9/mail/auto-reply)

To set your 
- Rules to Forward emails
- Auto-replies for out of office etc
